Post 1

Project Overview
The aim of this project is to create an overview of immigration into the United States and investigate the effect it has on the economy. We will specifically examine the impact of immigration on employment rate and wages of non-immigrants.

Hypothesis
We hypothesize that as immigrants, both legal and illegal, entered United States, the non-immigrants unemployment rate increased and wage decreased, especially of low-skilled workers.

Collected Data
So far we have collected immigration data from the years 1998 to 2015, organized by year and the continent the immigrants came from. We also have a data on number of legal permanent residents per year.
